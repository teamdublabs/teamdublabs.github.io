The studio

Team Dub Labs Ltd works where systems engineering meets frontier AI. Most shops do one or the other — publish papers, or build to spec. We take the hard part in between: the unproven, the unsolved, the thing with no off-the-shelf answer — and we make it run, locally, in production.

Then we carry it all the way to a system you can run. The lab is where the hard problems get solved. The studio is how they reach you.

What we do

01

Systems Architecture & Engineering

End-to-end product architecture, custom networking protocols, and localized deployment. We design and build robust infrastructure capable of handling intensive workloads.

02

Advanced AI & Orchestration

Local LLMs, multi-agent systems, and retrieval pipelines wired into your own data — not someone else’s API. Intelligence that runs on your infrastructure.

03

Custom R&D Spikes

The deep-tech problems with no off-the-shelf answers. Custom evaluation harnesses, complex automation loops, and prototyping novel execution layers under tight technical constraints.

04

Validation & Red Teaming

Short, high-impact engagements to stress-test your system’s architecture, security boundaries, or AI evaluation pipelines before moving to wide-scale production.
